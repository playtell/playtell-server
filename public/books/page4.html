The seasons were changing on the Island of Sodor. The leaves had begun to change color, and the air was growing crisp. Thomas the Tank Engine was feeling restless.

"Summer is almost over, and I haven't had any real fun," he complained.

"You're a fussy little engine," replied Gordon. "We're not here to have fun. We're here to work."

Well, that didn't make Thomas feel any better.

"I'd rather be fussy and fun than bossy and boring!" he retorted.
